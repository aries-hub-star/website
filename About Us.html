About Us

The Bureau of Fire Protection (BFP) is an agency of the Department of the Interior and Local Government (DILG) in the Philippines. It is responsible for implementing national policies and programs on fire prevention, suppression, and control.

With its dedicated firefighters and modern firefighting equipment, the BFP strives to protect lives, properties, and the environment from the devastating effects of fire. The bureau also conducts fire safety inspections, promotes fire safety awareness, and provides training and education on fire prevention and emergency response.

At the BFP, our mission is to ensure the safety and well-being of the Filipino people by preventing and suppressing fire incidents. We are committed to upholding the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and service excellence.
